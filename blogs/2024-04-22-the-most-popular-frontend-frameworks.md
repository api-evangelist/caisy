Examines popular frontend frameworks in 2024, with React leading at roughly 50% of frontend job postings, Angular at 38%, and Vue.js at 11%. Discusses emerging alternatives like Svelte and Solid.js and advises choosing frameworks based on project requirements, team expertise, and performance needs.
